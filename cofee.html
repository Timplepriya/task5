COFEE

1.Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain Coffea species.
2.All fruit must be further processed from a raw material—the fruit and seed—into a stable, raw product; un-roasted, green coffee.
3.To process the berries, the seed is separated from the fruit to produce green coffee.
4.Green coffee is then roasted, a process which transforms the raw product (green coffee) into a consumable product (roasted coffee).
5.Roasted coffee is ground into a powder and mixed with water to produce a cup of coffee.
