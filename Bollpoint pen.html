PEN

1.A ballpoint pen dispenses an oil-based ink by rolling a small hard sphere.
2.Usually 0.5–1.2 mm and made of brass, steel, or tungsten carbide.
3.The ink dries almost immediately on contact with paper.
4.The ballpoint pen is usually reliable and comes in both inexpensive and expensive types.
5.It has replaced the fountain pen as the most common tool for everyday writing.
6.László Bíró patented the first commercially successful ballpoint pen.
